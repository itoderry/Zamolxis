# Translate

- Common languages (Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Romanian, Dutch, etc.) — translate directly; you know them, no tool needed.
- Rare languages, scripts, or when unsure — `web_search "<phrase> in <language>"` and use a reliable result.

## Rules
- Preserve meaning and tone; keep names/numbers. If a word is ambiguous, give the most likely translation and note alternatives.
- Don't fake a language you don't actually know — say so and search instead.
